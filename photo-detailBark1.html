Iron Dog Bk. 1

For a few years now my father has jokingly told me that I needed to make an Iron Man suit for our dog and I always responded that as soon as I found a model for one I'd get right on it. Thus, when I saw the model online for Iron Dog Bark 1, I knew what I had to do. I printed and painted this model myself with the CAD work being done by MattiasHellberg. I gave the helmet to my father for this year's Father's Day and while it definetly would not fit my dog, the gift was still appreciated. The STLs can be found here: Iron Dog
