Iron Man Mk. 51 Prime Helmet

“Iron Man. That’s kind of catchy. It’s got a nice ring to it. I mean it’s not technically accurate. The suit’s a gold titanium alloy, but it’s kind of provocative…” (Tony Stark, Iron Man)

Inspired by sharp and sleek vehicals like the Lamborghini Aventador and F-22, the Iron Man Mk. 51 Prime helmet immediately drew me in with its hard edges and Knight-like appearance. This helmet was fully printed, painted, and assembled by myself with the CAD work being completed by Thingiverse user Budwin. The STLs can be found here: Iron Man Mk. 51 Prime Helmet
