Stark Infinity Guantlet

“And I…am…Iron Man”
(Tony Stark, Avengers: Infinity War)

Tony Stark’s infinity gauntlet is complete and added to my ever growing collection of Iron Man props. Featuring a repulsor palm light and glowing resin infinity stones. This project required me to learn about working with the unfamiliar medium of resin as well as negative mold making. In addition for this piece I was forced to adopt new painting techniques in order to get cleaner lines and richer colors. I did not model this project however all the painting, wiring, and resin work was done by me. All the CAD work for this project was done by DO3D. The original Iron Man trilogy served as my introduction into the world of Marvel and really kick started my interest in prop making as a whole. I want(ed) to become as close to a real world Tony Stark as possible and while realistically that is still a long ways off, I see the work I do now as a step in the right direction. You might also notice in these videos that I finally got around to adding the handsfree button doing the painting for the Mechanical Iron Man Mk. 3 Helmet.
