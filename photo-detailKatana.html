Tanjiro's Katana

“All I can do is work hard! That’s the story of my life!”
(Tanjiro Kamado, Kimetsu no Yaiba)

This 3D printed katana and sheath was painted and assembled to resemble Tanjiro’s Katana from the Anime Demon Slayer Aka Kimetsu no Yaiba. In order to achieve the metallic look of a real sword I had to learn about maximizing the effects of chrome spray paint and for that added authentic touch I wrapped the handle with real katana wrap material. The wrapping process was extremely difficult and took me numerous tries before I was even slightly happy with the result. All the CAD work on this project was performed by VEK3D. The STLs can be found here: Tanjiro's Katana
