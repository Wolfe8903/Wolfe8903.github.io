Levitating Planter

"We Never Lose Our Demons, Mordo. We Only Learn To Live Above Them."

This poseable Eye of Agamotto from Marvel's Dr. Strange was fully wired, assembled, and painted by myself with the CAD work being completed by Thigiverse user Black_Ram_Industries. The eye lids fully open and close and the light is togglable via a slide switch on the back. Due to the need for clarity in the infinity stone body I opted to use a Formlabs Form 3 resin 3D printer. Prior to this project SLA technology was entirely unfamilar to me and thus I was very challenged by learning the intricacies of this alternate printing process. The STLs can be found here: Eye of Agamotto
