Mask of The Yiga Clan

"You've got some nerve thinking you can go toe to toe with...MASTER KOHGA! I'll show YOU... YIGA ASSEMBLE! Me excluded of course."
(Master Kohga, Hyrule Warriors: Age of Calamity)

A very simple mask compared to some of my other work, this mask was printed in several pieces, painted and assembled by myself. All CAD work was completed by Cults user ALLTHEBUZ3D. I've always loved the very simplistic design of the Yiga's masks and how they feature a reversed eye of the Sheikah clan as a very obvious means of showing the player that they are the bad guys. I was extremely satisfied with the paint job on this project and found the model to be extremely well designed. The STLs can be found here: Mask of The Yiga Clan
