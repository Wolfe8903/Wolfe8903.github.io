About Joshua Lefkoff

Born and raised in Chattanooga, Tennesse, Joshua Lefkoff is currently seeking his Bachelor of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Tennessee. A highly motivated individual, Joshua is looking to develop a carreer within the field of additive manufacturing and aid in the future development of additive technologies. Joshua enjoys any oppourtunity to work with his hands and is always ready to try out new techniques and processes in the production of his projects.

Since 2018 Joshua Lefkoff has been a proud member of multiple rowing teams and clubs including The Baylor School Men's Crew Team and Crew Club at The University of Tennessee Knoxville. In 2022 Joshua recieved Baylor Crew's Spirit and Dedication award and in the years following has garnered, with aid from teamates, Tennessee Crew medals in multiple regattas.

As the former treasurer and current vice-president of SLOPE (Service Learning and Outreach with 3D Printers for Engineers) at the University of Tennesse, Joshua loves to share additive technologies with others. With the goal of the club to engage local knoxville elementary schools with STEM topics, particularly 3D printing, Joshua takes pride in engaging with his community in a meaningful way that also encourages growth within STEM fields.

Since he was young, Joshua has always enjoyed working in the kitchen with his mother. From brownies for his elementary school class to homemade pasta with his cousin, he has had a love of the culinary arts for as long as he can remember. Naturally from this came an interest in cooking competion shows, such as Holiday Baking Championship and Chopped. Thus, when offered information on applications to The Food Network's Rachel Vs. Guy Kids Cookoff he immediately sought to apply. Unfortunately, he was not accepted, however he persevered and continued to hone his skills. One year later the casting studio reached out once more to offer him a chance to apply, this time to Food Network's Chopper Junior. He siezed the chance and was ultimately accepted. Joshua trained for the following months under the Chef Amanda Varnell, formerly of Dish t' Pass, and finally he and his family were flown up to New York for filming.
Joshua was featured on Chopped Junior Season 3 Episode 8 titled Curry Hurry.
