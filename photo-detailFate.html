Helmet of Fate

"Your thoughts are better spent on the things you can change, not those you can't."
(Doctor Fate, Black Adam)

Based on the Doctor Fate helmet design from the game Injustice 2, this helmet was fully painted, wired, and assembled by myself with modeling being completed by VillainousPropShop. The gold film for the eyes was the biggest challenge in this project and I had to use thin PETG sheets in order to give them a firm backing. The helmet is big enough to be worn, however I opted not to for the showcase as the visibility is very poor. The STLs can be found here: Helmet of Fate
