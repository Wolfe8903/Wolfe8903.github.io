UTK Iron Man Mask

Based on my previous work with the Baylor Iron Man Faceplate, I felt it only fitting to show my new home, The University of Tennesse Knoxvile, my volunteer spirit by printing and painting a new faceplate. This Iron Man Mk. 42 faceplate is once again decorated similarly to the helmets of the school's football team with metallic orange vinly stickers and glowing orange eyes.
