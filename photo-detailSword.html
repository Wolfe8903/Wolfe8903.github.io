Master Sword

“…courage need not be remembered, for it is never forgotten."
(Princess Zelda, The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild)

This light up model of The Master Sword along with the scabbard from The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess took nearly an entire summer to complete. Both the scabbard and sword due to their size had to be printed in multiple pieces and then assembled, sanded, and painted. The initial CAD work on this project was completed by Thingiverse user ChaosCoreTech, however, another user, mistic100, remixed the model to allow for a neopixel setup similar to Adafruit's 3D Printed Guardian Sword. This project was particularly challenging in that it required me to work with airbrushing paints. I couldn't find a spray paint in the proper shade of gold and opted to airbrush over hand painting the piece as airbrushing resulted in a much cleaner and even distribution. I used a large cut up leather belt to secure the whole piece over my sholder. The STLs can be found here: ChaosCoreTech's Sword, mistic100's Remixed Sword, ChaosCoreTech's Scabbard , and Wiring Setup / Adafruit 3D Printed Guardian Sword
