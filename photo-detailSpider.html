Mechancial Spiderman Mask

“With great power comes great responsibility”
(Ben Parker, Spiderman)

The mechanical Spiderman mask, created in the Far From Home mask style, was wired, assembled and programed by myself. It features individual and dual eye movement functionality as well as a hands free trigger mechanism. One of the biggest challenges I faced in this project was in changing the trigger mechanism. CoolhelmetStudio completed the CAD work for this project and in their file set (linked below) they opted to use infrared sensors in the cheek area as the trigger. I found these too unreliable and chose to entirely rework the code to work with a limit switch in the chin instead. The STLs can be found here: Mechanical Spiderman Mask
