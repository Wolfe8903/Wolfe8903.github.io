Double Helix Lamp

After seeing the success of my Power T Neon Sign I knew I wanted to use what leftovers I had of the LEDs in another project. While browsing online I came across an image of what I can only assume was some sort of helical noble gas display and was struck with the idea for this desk lamp. Fully modeled, wired, and printed by myself this desk lamp displays an entertaining and unique springy double Helix design. One of the biggest challenges in this project was getting the body itself to print. Overhangs and 3D printing often do not play nice and thus coils are always a struggle. After some tinkering with the model orientation I finally found a way to print the model on its side and with supports that resulted in minimal issues. The STLs can be found here: Double Helix Lamp
