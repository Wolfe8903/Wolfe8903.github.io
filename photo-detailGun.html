"Wingchester" Lever Action Foam Dart Launcher

This 3D printed Lever Action Foam Dart Launcher was Printed and assembled by myself using the hardware kit and CAD files provided by Shellington Labs. This model caught my eye due to its almost futuristic look, alongside featuring a 1/4' thick laser cut steel lever and ~13kg spring based firing mechanism. In addition, the launcher offers real side loading capabilities, lever driven round loading, empty shell ejection, and a picatinny rail. The STLs and hardware kit can be found here: "Wingchester" Lever Action Foam Dart Launcher
