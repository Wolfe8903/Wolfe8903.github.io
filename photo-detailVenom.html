Venom Cowl

“We are venom.”

An update to the Mechanical Spiderman Mask, now with optional venom cowl. While not the crazy degree of engineering typically shown by my work I saw this model and knew it would be a perfect match to the existing mask. While not modeled by myself I did perform all the painting, printing, and scaling to fit my existing mask. This was a fun opportunity to revisit an old project and see what I could do to add that extra flair. The STLs can be found here: Venom
