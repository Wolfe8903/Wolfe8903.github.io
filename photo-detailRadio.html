Portal Radio Bluetooth Speaker

“I been thinken'. when life gives you lemons, don't make lemonade! get mad! I dont want your damn lemons! Do you know who I am?!? I'm the man who's gonna' burn your house down! with the lemons!”
(Cave Johnson, Portal 2)

This Bluetooth speaker housed in a 3D printed body was designed to look like the radio from Portal 2. All the wiring and painting for this project was completed by myself along with a small amount of modications to the original CAD files, created by MyMiniFactory user Alex I, to allow for a functional volume knob and larger speakers. This project was particularly challenging becasue I was entirely unfamiliar with the intricacies of Bluetooth technology and had never previously worked with speakers. The profile for the project also posed a challege as many of the electronics were very large and had to be precisely placed to acheive the desired functions.
