Cutting Board

A deviation from my usual plastic medium I decided it was about time I developed my woodworking skills and at the same time create something I could use in the kitchen. This cutting board consists of Ribbon Striped Magony, Paduk, and Curly Maple respectively and was trimmed to feature a filleted edge on the top and bottom faces. As wood was such an unfamiliar medium, this project posed many new challenges to me. I managed to perservere with the thought that the final product would be something I could cherish and truly utilize in day to day life.
