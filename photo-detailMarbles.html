Marble Run

Standing at almost 4 feet tall this marble run is entirely 3D printed apart from the motor, power supply, wires, and of course the marbles. This tower was fully assembled and painted by myself with all the CAD work being completed by Luka from Out Of Marbles. The modules at each stage can be removed and reorganized and there are a wide variety of other modules that I did not include in my tower. The STLs can be found here: Out Of Marbles
